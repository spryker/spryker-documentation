Company Unit Addresses

To open this page, go to Company Account>Company Unit Addresses page.

This page is where you can add and manage company unit addresses. Generally, you need to create company unit addresses after company business units have been created.

From this page you can:

  • Create Company Unit Addresses
  • Edit Company Unit Addresses

Graphic User Interface

The Company Unit Addresses page consists of the following elements:

# Element Description
1 +Create Company Unit Address This button adds a new company unit address. See Creating a New Company Unit Address for more details.
2 Show [number] entries Here you can select how many items you want to see in the list of navigation elements (10, 25, 50, 100).
3 Company Unit Address ID Numeric identifier of the company unit address.
4 Country Specifies the country in the address.
5 City Specifies the city in the address.
6 Zip Code Postal code.
7 Company The name of the company the address belongs to.
8 Address Contains the name of the street.
9 Number Numeric identifier of the building in the street.
10 Addition to Address Extra information that can accompany the address
11 Labels Displays the labels that are added to the address.
12 Actions In this column you can Edit a company unit address. See Managing Business Unit Addresses for more details.
13 Search Search and filter company business units. Start typing a business unit ID, company name or business unit name to filter the results and display them by ID, Company Name, Business Unit Parent and Business Unit Name in the List of Company Business Units table.
14 Showing [number 1] to [number 2] of [number 3] entries Shows how many entries ([number 1] to [number 2]) are displayed in the table. [number 2] depends on the value set in Show [number] entries (2), [number 3] represents total number of entries in the company business units table.
15

Previous [page number] Next

[page number] is the number of the page you are currently on. Here you can switch to the previous page or to the next page.

Creating a new Company Unit Address

To create a new company business unit, do the following:

  1. Click +Create Company Unit Address in the top right corner.
  2. On Create Company Unit Address page:
    • Select Company and Country from the dropdown
    • Enter City, Zip Code, Street to complete the address
  3. Click Save. The newly created company unit address will appear in the Company Unit Addresses table on Company Unit Addresses page.

Managing Company Unit Addresses

The company roles can be managed by triggering certain actions in the Actions column in the Company Unit Addresses table on Company Unit Addresses page. Specifically, company roles can be Edited there.

Editing Company Unit Address

Click Edit Company Unit Address in Actions column if you want to change address details such as the Company the address belongs to, Country, City, Zip Code, Street, Number of the building, Additinal Information for the address, Comments, and address Labels.
