Partner Information ABOUT CHANNELPILOT ChannelPilot is an innovative, cloud-based marketing solution for online merchants to increase their website traffic and reach. Companies are able to present their products on over 2,000 sales channels across more than 40 countries, for example Google, Yahoo, Bing, affiliate networks, marketplaces such as Amazon and eBay, in addition to social media. The tool goes beyond listing by providing merchants with the means to analyse, optimise and control performance, channels and products based on actionable KPIs. Additional modules include market price analysis and dynamic pricing.
